Protection plan

Coverage with cleaner terms

Single vision plan purchase from $20. Progressive plan purchase from $40. Claims, exclusions, lens only coverage, and frame warranty details are organized into simpler content blocks.

What is covered

  • Scratch, damage, or breakage for Vision X HD lenses
  • Parts and labour
  • Up to 3 uses during the coverage period
  • Single vision claim pricing from $39
  • Progressive claim pricing from $69

What is not covered

  • Loss or theft
  • Misuse, neglect, or unauthorized repairs
  • Contact lenses, cases, and accessories
  • Add ons like transitions, blue blocker, tints, or mirror coatings

Frame warranty notes

  • Frames are not covered by the protection plan itself
  • Manufacturer warranty applies for 1 year
  • $29 shipping if sent for inspection
  • Approved replacement frame is free
